Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Quit Tracker, We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard it. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

Quit Tracker does not collect any personal information from users. The app operates entirely on your device using local storage (UserDefaults) to store non-personal data such as the time since you quit your addiction, your milestones, and your money saved. We do not track, store, or share any personal or sensitive data.

Data Usage

Any data stored by Quit Tracker is used solely to enhance your experience. The data is stored only locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties. We do not use any third-party services or analytics to collect user data.

Third-Party Services

Quit Tracker does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or any external data collection services. There are no embedded tracking mechanisms such as cookies or device identifiers. Your data stays private and solely within the app on your device.

Security

Although Quit Tracker does not collect personal data, we still encourage users to secure their devices with a password, biometric authentication (such as Face ID or Touch ID), or other forms of security to prevent unauthorized access to the app and the local data it stores.

Your Rights

Since Quit Tracker does not collect or store personal data, you do not have to manage, delete, or modify your data in any way. You have the right to uninstall the app at any time, which will remove all local data stored on your device. You can also contact us for further information regarding privacy concerns.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed of any updates.
